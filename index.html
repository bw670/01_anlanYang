Has the world become more humorous or have I become happier?

PANDACOOL is a “depression culture” themed dynamic graphic image design work. With panda as its basic form, it integrates such elements as artistic expression, interactive effect, emotional expression to present a funnier and more humorous design effect, thus helping people release the negative emotions.


“Depression culture” refers to the language, text or picture with implication of decadence, despair, pessimism that are widely popular among the youth today.


“Depression culture” has gradually become a new way of communication among the youth who banter with their own lives and complain about the heavy workload of work and studies, the pressure of life, setback of love and so on in a humorous way. It embodies the spiritual characteristics of the contemporary youth as well as their humorous and generous mindset in the face of the huge pressure and anxiety.
